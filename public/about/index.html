About

When researching for my first offering of a programming course at IIT Gandhinagar, I was quickly overwhelmed by (and also grateful for) all the great resources that I could find to learn Python.

However, I was in a situation where the classes were held in a non-lab environment (i.e: no computers). I considered the possibility of making every class a live coding session, and potentially recording screencasts to share with the class.

However, after watching the the lectures from the NPTEL course on Programming, Data Structures and Algorithms in Python, I realized that creating slides was perhaps a more organized way of delivering the material.

I explored using Pythontutor and embedding REPLs from REPL.it in the class.
